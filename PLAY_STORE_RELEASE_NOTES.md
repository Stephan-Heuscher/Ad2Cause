## Notes for Play Store Submission

**Recommended:** Use the "Standard Format" or "Feature List Format" for most submissions.

**Character Limits by Platform:**
- Google Play Store: 500 characters (recommended), up to 4000 characters max
- Some regions: May display only first 80 characters initially

**Best Practices:**
- Lead with user benefits, not technical details
- Use positive, action-oriented language
- Highlight 3-5 key changes maximum
- Keep it scannable (use bullet points or short paragraphs)
- Avoid technical jargon
- Consider your target audience

**Version Consistency:**
- Ensure version number (1.0.2) matches build.gradle versionName
- Build number (3) matches build.gradle versionCode
